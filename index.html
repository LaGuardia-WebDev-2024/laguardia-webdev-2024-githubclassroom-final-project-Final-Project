Rowelle Barfield

Rowelle Barfield was raised in Brooklyn NY. She is a current senior in high school and is a Poet, she started writing when she was 13 as way she could process the world around her. She started spoken word in highschool and performed at the Bitter End, The Poet House, and Laguardia's "Rising Stars". She hopes to keep performing and is in the process of getting her first book published "Sharp Bones and Soft Skin”
